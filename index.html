ABOUT $SAO

Kawausosuki (かわうそ好き) is a Japanese artist known for cute, expressive otter illustrations that blew up on Twitter/X and LINE. The name means "otter lover" and their blobby, relatable otter became a viral icon of kawaii chaos. Kawausosuki replied with an otter drawing to a Grok Imagine video posted by Dogecoin creator Shibetoshi. Elon Musk reposted it with the caption: “Grok Imagine is super fun.” That moment launched Kawausosuki’s otter into global meme fame.
